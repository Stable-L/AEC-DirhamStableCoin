About AE Coin

AE Coin (AEC) is the world's first Dirham stable coin cryptocurrency asset built on the TRON blockchain, inspired by the United Arab Emirates Dirham.

Inspired by the UAE Dirham, AE Coin combines value stability, blockchain transparency, and transaction efficiency to support digital payments, asset transfers, and seamless integration within the Web3 ecosystem.

Note: AE Coin (AEC) is a digital asset inspired by the Dirham and does not represent legal tender or a central bank-issued currency.
